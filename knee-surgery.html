Biography

Dr. Peterson is a fully board certified, active duty orthopaedic surgeon in the US Navy, who is fellowship-trained in Hip and Knee Replacements.

She has a passion for restoring function, healthy aging, and longevity, and partners with her patients to decide whether surgery is the best next step for them to take on their joint replacement journey.

Clinical Expertise: avascular necrosis of the hip, complex and revision total joint replacement, hip arthritis, hip arthroplasty, knee arthritis, knee arthroplasty, and unicompartmental knee arthroplasty.
